DRIVE for Success

We are a registered Non-Profit Organisation dedicated to transforming lives through accessible healthcare, climate action, and community empowerment. Our focus is on Taxi Operators, Street Vendors, and daily commuters across Tshwane's major taxi ranks.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Our Vision 2028

To transform the Taxi Industry into a powerful engine for Health and Climate Change Resilience by 2028, ensuring all major Taxi Ranks in Tshwane are hubs for HIV Prevention, Treatment and accessible care, with clean water banking, cooling areas, and empowered workforce.

Our Mission

To provide accessible High quality HIV and Wellness services directly to Taxi Operators, Street Vendors and daily commuters through Fixed Walk-In Health Hubs at major Taxi Ranks: Marabastad, Bosman, Bloedmall, Mabopane Interchange and Donneboom Taxi.

Health Campaigns

Driving wellness across Tshwane's taxi industry

1 Million HIV/TB Campaign

Ambitious initiative reaching taxi operators, street vendors, and commuters with comprehensive prevention, testing, and linkage to care services across 5 major ranks.

Stigma-Free Mobile Units

Confidential wellness services delivered directly at taxi ranks, ensuring no driver or vendor is left behind in the fight against HIV and TB.

Health Education Drives

Interactive workshops on prevention, treatment adherence, and healthy lifestyles tailored for the transport sector workforce.

Quarterly Wellness Weeks

Intensified screening, ART distribution, and TB symptom screening events in partnership with Department of Health and Wits RHI.

Transforming Lives

Real impact stories & community upliftment

Greening Taxi Ranks

Over 500 trees planted at major taxi ranks, improving air quality and creating cooling zones for commuters and vendors.

Economic Empowerment

Skills training for 120+ street vendors in financial literacy and small business management, boosting household incomes.

HIV-Positive Drivers Support

Peer support groups for taxi drivers living with HIV, ensuring treatment adherence and reducing stigma within the industry.

Clean Water Access

Installation of water stations at 3 taxi ranks, providing free clean drinking water to 8,000+ daily commuters.
